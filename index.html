Overview of Google

Google is an American made search engine company, which was founded by Sergey Brin and Larry Page in 1998. Now, in 2024 more than 70% of all online searches are sent through Google. Google is one of the biggest tech companies in the world that nearly anyone can recognize, which is most known for its search engine, which helps people find information online, and is used daily by almost everyone. But Google does more than just handle searches. It also operates through advertising, and offering cloud computing services, creating software like Android, and additionally developed hardware like Google smartphones, Google assistants, and security features. Google’s main goal is "to organize the world’s information and make it universally accessible and useful".
