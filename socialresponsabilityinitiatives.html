Google's Social Responsibility Initiatives

Carbon Neutral Since 2007

Google has been carbon neutral since 2007, offsetting its emissions by investing in renewable energy. By 2030, Google aims for all data centers and offices to run entirely on clean energy, reducing greenhouse gas emissions and benefiting the environment.

Google.org

Google.org donates over 100 million USD annually, supporting education, disaster relief, and innovative solutions like AI for healthcare and environmental challenges.

Grow with Google

This initiative helps people learn vital digital skills, focusing on underrepresented groups, increasing employability, and aiding economic mobility.
