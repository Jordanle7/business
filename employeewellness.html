Google Employee Wellness Programs

Google is known for treating its employees really well. It’s often listed as one of the best places to work because of its great pay, work hours, and lots, and lots of benefits. Google also has a strong focus on diversity and inclusion, working to make sure women, people of color, and others from different groups have equal opportunities.



Mental Health Support

Google offers free therapy and counseling services to its employees, such as Onsite wellness centers.

Fitness and Health

Employees have access to on-site gyms, fitness programs, and medical services such as Medical, dental, and vision insurance. Healthy meals are also provided in Google’s cafeterias.

Flexible Working

Since the pandemic, Google has allowed many employees to work from home, giving them more flexibility in balancing work and life, for example in certain circumstances Google will allow employees to work wherever they believe they will get the most work done.
